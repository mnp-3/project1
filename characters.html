♡ Some charcters in the Magnolia Parks Universe ♡

Magnolia
Parks

The glamorous London IT-girl, known for her impeccable fashion, sharp wit, and magnetic presence, she lives in a whirlwind of high society drama, privilege, and heartbreak. However, beneath her polished exterior, Magnolia struggles with vulnerability, deep insecurities, and an all-consuming love for BJ Ballentine.

Baxter James
Ballentine

Also known as BJ and Beej. Is Magnolia’s on-again, off-again love. Charming, reckless, and fiercely loyal yet deeply flawed. His relationship with Magnolia is as destructive as it is passionate, defining much of his story.

Daisy
Haites

Vibrant, bold, and fiercely independent. Torn between her privileged social life and the dangerous world of her family, she embodies a mix of lighthearted charm and heavy responsibility. Her story is one of survival and resilience.

Christian
Hemmes

Calm, collected counterpoint to the chaos of Magnolia and BJ. Intelligent and ambitious, he offers stability and reason, but his own complicated feelings and choices weave him tightly into the drama of the group.

Julian
Haites

Daisy’s protective older brother, feared for his ruthless involvement in London’s criminal underworld. Beneath the violence lies a fierce loyalty to his family, though his choices constantly put Daisy in danger.

Bridget
Parks

Magnolia’s younger sister, is steady, warm, and far less entangled in chaos than Magnolia. She offers a grounding presence in the Parks family, balancing elegance with quiet strength.

Henry
Ballentine

BJ’s younger brother, is disciplined, ambitious, and determined to step out of the shadow of BJ’s reckless reputation. His pragmatic nature often clashes with the turbulence around him.

Jonah
Hemmes

Christian’s older brother, is witty, easygoing, and drawn to fun. While he doesn’t always take life as seriously as Christian, his charm and good humor make him a staple of their world.

Taura
Sax

Best friends with Magnolia, enigmatic and loyal but quick to challenge others, she adds both tension and energy to the tight-knit circle.

Killian
Tiller

Also known as Tills. Sharp, determined detective entangled in the Parks and Haites world. Focused, persistent, and unafraid to dig into dangerous truths,
